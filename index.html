Whenever you want to block

Visit a page and click the bookmark

Go to any of these pages on X and click "Collect & Block":

  • x.com/username/followers
  • x.com/username/verified_followers
  • Open a post → click Reposts

It scrolls, collects, and blocks everyone. When done, the list is copied to your clipboard.
