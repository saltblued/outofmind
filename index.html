Block followers or reposters

One bookmarklet does everything. Set it up once, use it whenever.

Step 1 — Add the bookmarklet

Drag this to your bookmarks bar

Once it's in your bookmarks bar, you can reuse it anytime. Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible.

Collect & Block
northDrag the button above into your bookmarks bar. Don't click it here — drag it.
Step 2 — Go to X and click the bookmark

Open a follower or reposter page

Go to the account you want to block and open their follower page (x.com/username/followers). Or open any post and tap Reposts to view reposters.

Once the list of people is showing, click "Collect & Block" in your bookmarks bar. It will scroll through the page and block automatically. When done, it copies the usernames to your clipboard.

info Good to knowX doesn't always load every follower — the bookmarklet collects what's visible on the page. Repost pages tend to load faster and fully.
Step 3 — Publish (optional)

Share the list with the community

After blocking, the accounts are on your clipboard. Paste them below to create a public blocklist others can install.
